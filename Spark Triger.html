New Orders SPARK Triggers

When customer buys product from your marketplace, we will capture below events and apply tags to the customer record.

Order Placed and customer successfuly paid the order - Customer will receive below tags

1. Business --> {business name}. This will help you to segment your customer records coming from UnifieCom
2. Region --> {Region}. This will help you to segment your customer records coming from UnifieCom and which Region.
3. Product --> {SKU} where SKU is the SKU of the product purchased
4. Order Status --> PURCHASED . You now know that customer has paid and can start building communication like sending order email.
5. Order Status --> {SKU}_PURCHASED. Here SKU will be the SKU of the product. This will help you to trigger automation specific to the purchase of this product. 

Order is now Shipped - Customer will receive below tags

1. Order Status --> SHIPPED . You now know that customer has paid and can start building communication like sending order Shipping email or asking for feedback.
2. Order Status --> {SKU}_SHIPPED. Here SKU will be the SKU of the product. This will help you to trigger automation specific to the purchase of this product. 

Returned Order SPARK Triggers

This will help you when customer requested for the refund and product is returned to warehouse. UnifieCom will apply the trigger and give you the return information to engage your unhappy customers.

1. Order Status --> RETURNED
2. Order Status --> {SKU}_RETURNED
